Nanyang Siang Pau profiled David Justin's journey from self-taught crypto enthusiast to Guinness World Record-holding educator — anchored by his Mandarin-language teaching style that has made public-blockchain fundamentals accessible to tens of thousands of learners across Southeast Asia's Chinese-speaking communities.
