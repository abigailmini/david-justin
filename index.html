About David Justin

From Student to
World Record Holder

David Justin is Malaysia's most recognised blockchain educator — a man who turned his passion for crypto into a movement that has transformed hundreds of thousands of lives across Asia.

On November 13, 2022, David made history in Kuala Lumpur by breaking the Guinness World Record for the Largest Cryptocurrency Lesson — gathering an unprecedented crowd of learners to prove that financial education belongs to everyone.

Since then, he has been featured by Bitget as a top educator in Asia, awarded the Asia Top 100 Brand Honesty Award, and grown a community of over 890,000 newsletter subscribers. With more than a decade in the industry, his programs have earned a 99.9% satisfaction rate.
