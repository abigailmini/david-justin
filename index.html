The Story

From Passion to
World Record Holder

David Justin is Malaysia's #1 blockchain educator — a Guinness World Record holder for orchestrating the world's Largest Cryptocurrency Lesson with 897 participants on 13 November 2022 in Kuala Lumpur.

As the No.1 Best-Selling Author of World of Cryptocurrencies and founder of the Six-Star Course (六星课程), David has educated hundreds of thousands of people across Malaysia and Asia on how to safely navigate the digital asset revolution.

With 890K+ YouTube subscribers, a Bitget Featured Educator badge, and the Asia Top 100 Brand Honesty Award, David Justin's mission is simple: give every individual the knowledge to create financial freedom through blockchain.
