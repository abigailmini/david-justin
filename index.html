From Passion to
World Record Holder

David Justin is Malaysia's most recognised blockchain educator — a professional cryptocurrency trader, author, and entrepreneur who has dedicated his career to advancing structured education in digital assets.

Since entering the cryptocurrency space in 2017, David has educated individuals from Malaysia, Singapore, Taiwan, China, Hong Kong, and South Africa. His work is grounded in real market participation across multiple cycles — not theoretical speculation.

On 13 November 2022, David made history by achieving the Guinness World Record for the Largest Cryptocurrency Lesson — 897 participants in a single session in Kuala Lumpur.

He is the No.1 Best-Selling Author of World of Cryptocurrencies, a Bitget Featured Educator, and recipient of the Asia Top 100 Brand Honesty Award.
